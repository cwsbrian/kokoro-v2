# Kokoro Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** February 25, 2025  
**App name:** Kokoro  
**Package:** com.cwsbrian.kokoro (Android) / com.cwsbrian.kokoro (iOS)

---

## 1. Overview

Kokoro (the "App") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what information the App collects, uses, and stores, for what purposes, and how it is shared with third parties and protected.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information (when you sign in)

- **Google Sign-In:** Email address, display name, profile photo URL, and other basic information provided by your Google account.
- Sign-in is **optional**. You can use core features (reading and swiping) without signing in.

### 2.2 App Usage Data (signed-in users only)

- **Reading state:** Swipe response count, last analysis timestamp, AI analysis results.
- **Swipe history:** Poem IDs, responses (e.g., like/dislike), timestamps.
- This data is used for **cross-device sync** and **restoration after reinstall**.

### 2.3 Profile Image (avatar)

- **Profile photos** set by signed-in users may be stored on your device and in the cloud (Firebase Storage).
- Used only for sync and restoration.

### 2.4 Automatically Collected Information

- **Firebase:** When using authentication, Firestore, or Storage, data is processed through Google Firebase. For Firebase’s practices, see [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Firebase Terms of Service](https://firebase.google.com/terms).

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Service delivery:** Storing reading history, providing AI-based analysis, syncing across devices.
- **Account management:** Verifying identity via Google Sign-In and linking profile/avatar.
- **Service improvement:** (If analytics are used) Improving app stability and usability with anonymized usage statistics.

---

## 4. Third-Party Sharing and Services

- **Google / Firebase:** We use Google Firebase for authentication (Auth), database (Firestore), and file storage (Storage). Data is processed according to Google’s policies and the region configured for the Firebase project.
- **Third-party advertising:** We do not sell or share personal information for third-party targeted advertising.

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Retention:** Data linked to your account is retained while your account remains active.
- **Account deletion:** You can request deletion of data (reading state, avatar, etc.) by revoking the App’s access in your Google account or by contacting the developer.
- **Local device data:** Data stored on your device (e.g., AsyncStorage) is removed when you sign out or uninstall the App. Data already synced to the cloud may require a separate deletion request.

---

## 6. Security

- We use encryption in transit (HTTPS), Firebase security rules, and access controls so only authenticated users can access their own data.
- We cannot guarantee absolute security over the internet or cloud services.

---

## 7. Your Rights

- **Access, correction, deletion:** You may request to view, correct, or delete your data.
- **Withdraw consent:** Signing out or uninstalling the App stops further collection and use. To delete data already stored, please contact us using the details below.

---

## 8. Children

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.
